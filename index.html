About Diana

Diana Bechdol is a current graduate student at SUNY Albany’s Rockefeller College studying Public Policy. In 2020, she created the K-12 art program at Troy Prep Charter Schools, where she subsequently taught high school art for 3 years. She then worked as a Therapeutic Art Instructor at a residential school for girls with learning disabilities who struggle with their mental health. Currently, her research examines how mental health policy and special education policy impact students' educational experiences.
